This Code Together event is a time for you to work alongside and connect with other people in your local community.

Now, it's important to note that this isn't a class, so there won't be a teacher, but it is your opportunity to talk with other people and get help jumpstarting your learning process or with a programming lesson / personal project you've been working on.

What are these meetups all about?

These gatherings aren't classes, so there won't be a teacher, but it is your opportunity to talk with other beginners, mentors, and leaders in your local community. You'll get help jumpstarting the learning process or tackling that programming lesson / personal project you've been working on.
